Naresh Bello

Building Legos as a kid, ignited a passion for harmony in structural design. Telling stories through colors and shapes sparked a passion and drive for the fine arts and the esthetics of good design. Working with a team to redesign the registration page for The Kindness Project, an organization that supports foster families, allowed me to express my vision of a fluid design. Skills required for this project included Visual Design, Visual Prototyping, Wireframing, and User Interface Development allowing users to smoothly navigate the website via mobile phone.
